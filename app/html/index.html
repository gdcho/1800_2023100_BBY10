Welcome to Ecocentives

A web-based application to encourage Canadians to practice environmentally-friendly habits with an incentive program, allowing users to complete recurring tasks while competing with one another to collect points for reward redemption.

Our Purpose

At Ecocentives, we believe that small actions can lead to big changes. By encouraging Canadians to practice environmentally-friendly habits through an incentive program, we hope to create a more sustainable future for our planet.
