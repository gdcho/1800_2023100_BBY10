About Us

Welcome to Ecocentives! We are a team of students who created this website as part of our CST COMP 1800 course project. Our mission is to promote sustainable living and environmental awareness through a rewards-based system.

We believe that small actions can make a big impact, and that everyone can contribute to a healthier planet. That's why we created Ecocentives - a platform that encourages and rewards environmentally friendly behaviors. By participating in our challenges and completing eco-friendly tasks, users can earn points that can be redeemed for various rewards.

We hope that Ecocentives will inspire and motivate people to adopt more sustainable practices in their daily lives. By working together, we can create a greener, cleaner, and healthier planet for generations to come. Thank you for joining us on this journey!
