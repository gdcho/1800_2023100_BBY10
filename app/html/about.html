About Us

At Ecocentives, we believe that small actions can lead to big changes. Our environmentally-focused incentive program rewards Canadians for making sustainable choices in their daily lives. Our goal is to make sustainable living accessible and rewarding for everyone. Join us today and start making a positive impact on the planet!

How it works:

Sign up: To get started, create an account on our platform using your email. It's completely free and easy to use.

Choose your actions: Browse our list of sustainable actions and choose the ones that work best for you. We offer a variety of actions, from reducing your energy usage to composting, to using alternative transportation. You can select the ones that align with your lifestyle and sustainability goals.

Track your progress: As you complete each action, provide proof of completion by uploading images. You'll be able to track your progress and see how your choices are making a positive impact on the environment. We'll provide you with personalized feedback and tips along the way to help you stay on track.

Earn rewards: For each action you complete, you'll earn Ecocentives points. These points can be redeemed for rewards like gift cards or donations to environmental organizations. The more actions you complete, the more points you'll earn and the greater the rewards you'll receive.
